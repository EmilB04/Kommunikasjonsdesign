The Game of Modes

This webpage demonstrates the storytelling modes from our lectures. Each section both uses the mode and explains how it was implemented. Together, these modes create the toolbox of digital storytelling.

Choose one of the modes below to explore its unique storytelling capabilities, or scroll down to explore them all.

01

Text

"The door had just been kicked in. Police outside shouted the word that would define the night: 'Knife!'"

Implementation

Text is the foundation of HTML and is added simply with <p> tags.

Why it's used

Text remains the most versatile and accessible storytelling medium. It allows for precise control of narrative pacing, complex ideas, and emotional nuance.

Effect on reader

Creates mental imagery and allows readers to consume at their own pace. The example uses dramatic present tense and specific details to create immediate tension.

02

Photo

A picture tells a thousand words

Implementation

Added with the <img> tag. Images are dynamically fetched from Picsum Photos.

Why it's used

Photos provide immediate visual context and emotional connection. They establish credibility and help readers visualize unfamiliar situations.

Effect on reader

Creates instant emotional response and improves comprehension. A well-chosen photo conveys mood, atmosphere, and information simultaneously.

03

Audio

Implementation

Embedded with <audio>. Audio adds ambience and conveys emotion through recorded voices.

Why it's used

Audio engages our most emotional sense and adds temporal dimension. Perfect for authentic voices and atmospheric storytelling.

Effect on reader

Creates immersive atmosphere and builds empathy. Makes listeners feel present at events through authentic audio experiences.

04

Video

Implementation

Inline video via <video>. Provides movement and context. Source: W3Schools.

Why it's used

Combines visual and auditory storytelling. Closest medium to real experience, perfect for demonstrating processes and capturing spontaneous moments.

Effect on reader

Provides comprehensive sensory experience and creates strong sense of "being there." Most engaging multimedia format available.

05

Map

Implementation

Embedded with Google Maps iframe. Maps anchor stories in specific locations and provide geographic context.

Why it's used

Provides spatial context that text cannot achieve. Helps readers understand distances, relationships, and geographic factors.

Effect on reader

Creates spatial understanding and enables interactive exploration. Transforms abstract locations into navigable spaces.

06

Timeline

19:00

Michelsen calls ER

20:30

Fire alarm starts

21:00

Police arrive

21:12

Three shots fired

Implementation

Ordered events presented as structured timeline to show sequence over time with visual progression.

Why it's used

Organizes complex sequences into chronological order. Essential for investigative journalism and historical narratives.

Effect on reader

Clarifies causality and creates dramatic tension. Precise timestamps build urgency and authenticity.

07

Data

data.json 
{
  "year": 2019,
  "police_killings": 6,
  "city": "Bergen"
}

Implementation

Raw data displayed in <pre> blocks to preserve structure and formatting for transparency.

Why it's used

Provides transparency and enables verification. Appeals to analytically-minded readers who want to examine evidence.

Effect on reader

Builds trust through transparency and provides concrete evidence. JSON format makes information easily scannable.

08

Chart

Sample Data Trends

Implementation

Generated via QuickChart API and embedded as responsive images to visualize complex numerical relationships.

Why it's used

Transforms numerical data into visual patterns. Reveals trends and relationships that are difficult to discern from raw numbers.

Effect on reader

Makes complex data immediately understandable and reduces cognitive load. Enables quick pattern recognition and comparison.

Note: Data shown is fictional for demonstration purposes only.

09

Infoviz

60% Mobile
30% Desktop
10% Tablet

Implementation

Generated dynamically with QuickChart.io. Infoviz condenses complex numbers into instantly understandable visual forms.

Why it's used

Transforms abstract data into intuitive representations. Makes statistical information accessible across all literacy levels.

Effect on reader

Provides instant understanding of proportional relationships and transcends language barriers through universal visual language.
